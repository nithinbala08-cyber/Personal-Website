BLOG4 | March 28th 2026
Title: TRM implementation scenerio

Key term: Tokenization
-> LLM tokenization is the process of breaking text into smaller units called tokens, such as words, subwords, or characters, so a language model can convert them into numbers and process them.

THIS is what makes LLMs expensive. THIS is the issue I want to tackle. 
The average engineer will use about $250k worth of tokens in a year, so let's go over a scenerio of a QA system, and how we can visually picture this model being used to reduce tokenization.

All in all: TRM eliminated irrelevent garbage -> reduces unnecessary tokenization -> corporate save money -> everyone happy
